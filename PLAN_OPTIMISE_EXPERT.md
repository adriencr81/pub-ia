### **4. NO LEAD MAGNET — GOING STRAIGHT TO PITCH**

**Current:**
```
Cold email → "Interested? Book call" → Landing with video demos
```

**Expert Approach:**
```
Lead magnet FIRST (free content with high perceived value)

Example email:
"Salut [Prénom],

I analyzed 100+ winning ads in [Sector] on Meta Ad Library.
Found 5 patterns competitors are missing.

→ Free breakdown: [sector]-winning-angles.pdf

No strings attached."

Benefits:
- Higher open rate (curiosity, not pitch)
- Self-qualification (they download = interested)
- Social proof via data (not just claims)
- Retargeting (email after they download)

Sequence:
Email 1 → "Free analysis download"
Wait 3 days
Email 2 → "Here's what we found..."
Wait 3 days
Email 3 → "Want to test the winning angles? 500€ sprint..."

Result: 60%+ conversion from cold email → call (not 20%)
```
