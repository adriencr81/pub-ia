## Quarterly Checkpoints

### Q1 (Semaine 1-13)
**Milestone:** 1-2 premiers clients payants

**Review:**
- Content consistency: 3 posts/week maintained?
- Engagement rate: Comments/likes trending up?
- Prospect quality: Are they right-fit?
- Sprint quality: Happy with deliverables?

**Adjust if needed:**
- Content not landing? Change pillar (e.g., more making-of)
- Prospects not converting? Refine qualification or pitch
- Sprints too slow? Find production speedups

### Q2 (Semaine 14-26)
**Milestone:** 2 retainers, 4-6 one-shots total, revenue 7-12k€

**Review:**
- Conversion rate: 500€ → retainer? (Target 30-40%)
- Content ROI: Which pillar drives engagement + DMs?
- Prospect pipeline: Sustainable inflow?
- Retention: Client satisfaction on debrief calls?

**Adjust if needed:**
- Retainer conversions low? Strengthen debrief process
- Content engagement low? Increase frequency or change format
- Churn starting? Identify reason (quality, communication, pricing)

### Q3 (Semaine 27-39)
**Milestone:** 4-6 retainers, 12-15k€/month base revenue

**Review:**
- Unit economics: What's your true $ margin per retainer?
- Acquisition cost: How much do you spend to get 1 retainer? (Time = cost)
- Operationsl scalability: Can you handle 1-2 more retainers solo?
- Content leverage: Is content generating qualified leads (% of new customers)?

**Adjust if needed:**
- Revenue plateau? You might be hitting solo capacity (5-6 retainers)
- Churn uptick? Maybe clients getting bored or wanting more (upsell?)
- Content fatigue? Switch to batch-and-schedule, outsource ideas

### Q4 (Semaine 40-52)
**Milestone:** 5-8 retainers, 15-18k€+/month, sustainable business

**Annual review:**
- Did you hit 45-55k€ CA? (Median scenario)
- How many clients did you turn down? (constraint = price, not availability)
- What would it take to 2x next year?
- Do you want to stay solo or build a team?

**Strategic choices:**
1. **Stay lean & profitable (solo path)**
   - Target: 5-6 retainers, 1-2 one-shots/month
   - Income: 18-20k€/month
   - Effort: 40h/week
   
2. **Build toward scale (team path)**
   - Hire 1 assistant (production/editing)
   - Target: 8-10 retainers + higher one-shot volume
   - Your role: Strategy + client relationships
   - Income: 30-40k€/month (split with team)

## Key Reminders

1. **Content before clients.** Spend weeks 1-8 building credibility. No shortcuts.

2. **The Day 7 debrief is your biggest lever.** This is where retainer conversions happen. Never skip it. Schedule it before delivering the sprint.

3. **80/20 on content.** 3 posts/week on LinkedIn will generate 80% of your inbound. Everything else (cold email, partnerships) is 20%.

4. **Pricing power comes from differentiation + proof.** Your first 2-3 testimonials unlock higher prices. Get them.

5. **Stay solo as long as possible.** You'll learn what clients need. Solo = flexible. Once you hire, you're locked into higher burn rate.

6. **The first retainer is the hardest.** After you have 2-3, conversion becomes systematic.
