## 🔴 Red Flags & Fixes

| Problem | Signal | Fix |
|---------|--------|-----|
| Content not landing | <1% engagement rate after 20 posts | Change format (video vs carousel) or timing |
| Prospects not responding | 0% DM response rate | Wait longer before DMing (more comments first) |
| Calls not converting | <20% of demos → contracts | Refine qualification or pitch (use call script from templates) |
| Sprints taking too long | >12 hours per sprint | Create production templates, reuse scripts, speed up editing |
| Too many refund requests | >1 unhappy client | Quality issue — spend more on hypothesis doc, be realistic on delivery |
| Feeling overwhelmed | 3–4 active retainers + new one-shots | You're at capacity. Decision: hire assistant or stop new business |

## 🎯 Decision Points

### Month 3 (After First Clients)
**Decision:** Keep scaling acquisition or optimize retention?

- **Path A (Growth):** Double down on content + outreach. Target 6–8 retainers by month 12.
- **Path B (Profit):** Stabilize at 3–4 retainers, 1–2 one-shots/month. Keep solo, maximize margin.

**My recommendation:** Path A for first 6 months, then reassess.

### Month 6 (Hitting Capacity)
**Decision:** Stay solo or bring in help?

- **Solo:** Max 5–6 retainers + 1–2 one-shots/month. Income: 15–20k€/month. Effort: 40h/week.
- **Hire assistant:** Can handle 8–10 retainers. Your role shifts to strategy + client relationships. Income: 25–35k€/month (after splitting cost). Effort: 50h/week initially, 30h/week after systems.

### Month 12 (Year 1 Review)
**Decision:** Expand, double down, or pivot?

- **Expand:** Build a 2–3 person studio. Target: 1,000€+ MRR. Next 12 months focus: hiring, systemization, positioning.
- **Double down solo:** Optimize margins. Target: 200–250k€/year net. Next 12 months focus: premium clients, higher retainer prices, less volume.
- **Pivot:** Productize (e.g., "Monthly Creative Autopilot" SaaS). Explore agency model with freelancers. Next 12 months focus: discovery.

### Common Questions Answered

**Q: Should I charge 500€ or 1,200€ for my first client?**
A: 500€ (launch price) for first 2 clients, in exchange for testimonials. Then 1,200€. See QUICK_START_TEMPLATES.md pricing tree.

**Q: How much content do I need before pitching?**
A: Minimum 12 posts (8 weeks of 3/week). You need presence first. See PLAN_EXECUTION_12_MOIS.md Phase 1.

**Q: When should I hire my first person?**
A: Month 6–8 if you're booked (3–4+ retainers + consistent one-shots). Bring in assistant for editing/production. See decision tree above.

**Q: Can I do this part-time?**
A: Phase 1 (weeks 1–8): 5–7h/week. Phase 2 (weeks 9–14): 10–15h/week (with client delivery). Phase 3+: 20–25h/week if you want revenue. You can't build this as a side project and expect month 2 revenue—it's not realistic.
