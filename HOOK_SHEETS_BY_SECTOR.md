## 🏥 SECTEUR 1: Nutrition Sportive

**Prospects:** Nutripure, Novoma, 4Endurance, Ergysport, Impulse Nutrition

### Hook 1: Before-After Transformation (BEST)
**Status:** ✅ Used by 5+ competitors, 3+ weeks active
**Used by:** Nutripure ads, 4Endurance, Ergysport
**Duration:** 3-4 weeks average
**Pattern:**
- 0-2s: Close-up "before" (tired/weak/unfit)
- 2-5s: Product shown briefly
- 5-8s: Close-up "after" (energized/strong/fit)
- 8-10s: CTA (link, discount code)

**Best for:** Muscle building, weight loss, energy, recovery
**Why works:** Visual proof, emotional journey, immediate understanding

**5 Variations:**
1. **Dramatic** - High contrast, quick cuts, dramatic music
2. **Scientific** - Slow-mo muscle, technical explanation overlay
3. **Lifestyle** - Person in gym, at work, feeling confident
4. **Emotional** - Close-up face, relief/joy expression
5. **Speed** - 2x pacing, fast cuts, energetic music

---

### Hook 2: Testimonial/Social Proof (STRONG)
**Status:** ✅ Used by 3+ competitors, 2+ weeks active
**Used by:** Novoma, Ergysport
**Duration:** 2-3 weeks
**Pattern:**
- 0-3s: Real person talking (credible, relatable)
- 3-7s: Their problem statement (before situation)
- 7-10s: Product use + result
- 10-12s: Recommendation/CTA

**Best for:** Trust-building, skeptical audiences, health claims
**Why works:** Human connection, authenticity, relatability

**5 Variations:**
1. **Customer testimonial** - Real person, authentic setting
2. **Expert endorsement** - Doctor/trainer talking
3. **Athlete proof** - Performance athlete result
4. **Daily user** - Relatable person (not model)
5. **Family angle** - Parent/child, community aspect

---

### Hook 3: Problem + Fast Solution (STRONG)
**Status:** ✅ Used by Jolt Recovery, 4Endurance
**Duration:** 2+ weeks
**Pattern:**
- 0-3s: Pain point shown (muscle soreness, fatigue, recovery time)
- 3-6s: Product introduced as solution
- 6-10s: Fast result/relief shown
- 10-12s: CTA

**Best for:** Acute problems (muscle recovery, energy crash, soreness)
**Why works:** Immediate relevance, problem-solution clarity, speed

**5 Variations:**
1. **Pain-focused** - Exaggerate problem for comedy/drama
2. **Speed-focused** - "Only 20 min to recover"
3. **Comparison** - With product vs without
4. **Lifestyle** - Problem in daily context (office, gym, bed)
5. **Humor** - Funny problem → easy solution

---

### Hook 4: Scientific/Data Authority (MODERATE)

**Pattern:**
