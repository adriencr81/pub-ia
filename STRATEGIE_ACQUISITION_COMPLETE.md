## 🎯 **Critical Success Factors**

### ✅ **1. Vidéos démo QUALITÉ**
- Not perfect (no budget), but CLEAR (good audio, clear hook)
- 15-30s each (short = higher completion)
- 3 secteurs différents (shows range)

### ✅ **2. Landing page CRÉDIBLE**
- Démos visibles (proof)
- Cas d'études cohérents (even if hypothetical, must be realistic)
- Calendly intégré (no friction)

### ✅ **3. Emails hyper-perso**
- NOT "hey I have a service"
- YES "I saw your [X ads], here's your bottleneck"
- Reference landing page (not just "come see me")

### ✅ **4. Consistent follow-up**
- 50% won't reply first time
- Day 5 follow-up = doubles response rate

### ✅ **5. Call script practiced**
- Know your angles
- Know their pain (from their Meta ads)
- Can close on call (don't over-think it)
