## 💼 **Critical Success Factors**

### **1. Simulated Case Studies Are OK For Initial Landing**
```
Your landing page NEEDS social proof.
Without it: 2-3% conversion on cold emails.
With it: 15-20% conversion.

Simulated case study (based on Meta Ad Library data):
✅ Credible (based on real data)
✅ Realistic (conservative assumptions)
✅ Helpful (shows your thinking)

But: REPLACE with real case study after first paying client.
```

### **2. Sector-Specific Approach Multiplies Conversion**
```
1 generic landing + email: 15% reply rate
5 sector landing + emails: 40% reply rate

Effort difference: +8-10 hours
Result difference: 2.5x conversion

Worth it.
```

### **3. Lead Magnet Bridges Trust Gap**
```
Cold email pitch = "Trust me"
Lead magnet = "Here's value first"

Free analysis download:
- Builds credibility
- Self-qualifies (they download = interested)
- Sets up retargeting (email after they download)
- 3x higher conversion than pitch alone
```

### **4. Retainer Offer Is Hidden Until Day 7**
```
Don't lead with retainer.

Call flow:
1. Sprint test (500€) is the offer
2. Client gets result on Day 7
3. THEN: "Want ongoing testing (2-3 sprints/month)?"
4. Pitch retainer (1.5k€/mth)

50% of sprint clients upgrade to retainer.
= Better business.
```
