## Email → Landing → Call → Production Flow

### Phase 1: Email Outreach
**Content:**
- Text only (5 lines max)
- Recognition: Specific ads visible on Meta Ad Library
- Problem: Production bottleneck, not budget
- Solution: 5 video variants, 72h
- **CTA: Link to landing page** (NOT video attachment)

**Why no video in email?**
- Better click-through rates
- Professionalism (landing page = credibility)
- Tracking (see who clicks)

---

### Phase 2: Landing Page (timeline-ai.fr)
**Content:**
- Hero: "5 angles créatifs en 72h"
- 1-2 video examples (validated with Arcads, matching sector)
- Problem → Solution → Pricing breakdown
- CTA → Calendly link

**Video examples:** Tested + validated before sending emails

---

### Phase 3: Discovery Call (15 min via Calendly)
**Input:**
- Client brief: 3-5 product photos/clips + pain point
- Audience + top 2-3 current ads for reference

**Output:**
- Agreement: 500€ for 72h sprint
- Delivery date confirmed

---

### Phase 4: Production Sprint (72h)
**Deliverable:** 5 video variants (same hook, different angles)

**NOT:** 5 different hooks/concepts  
**YES:** 5 variations of their winning hook

---

## The 5 Variations Model (CRITICAL)

### Example: Sports Nutrition
**Winning hook they use:** Before-After Transformation

**Why this hook:**
- ✅ Used by 5+ competitors (Nutripure, 4Endurance, Ergysport)
- ✅ Active 3+ weeks (proof it works)
- ✅ Meta Ad Library validated

**5 Variations:**
1. **Dramatic** — High contrast, quick cuts, dramatic music
2. **Scientific** — Slow-mo muscle, technical overlay
3. **Lifestyle** — Gym/work context, person confident
4. **Emotional** — Close-up face, relief/joy expression
5. **Speed** — 2x tempo, fast cuts, energetic

Same hook, 5 different angles. Client tests all 5 → sees which performs best.

---

### Example: Cosmetics
**Winning hook they use:** Before-After Skin

**5 Variations:**
1. **Acne clear** — Spots → clear skin
2. **Glow** — Dull → radiant
3. **Texture** — Rough → smooth
4. **Redness** — Irritated → calm
5. **Anti-aging** — Fine lines → firmer

Same before-after format, different skin problems addressed.

## Hook Selection Methodology

All hooks validated by **Meta Ad Library analysis:**

| Hook Status | Criteria | Example |
|------------|----------|---------|
| **BEST** | 5+ competitors using, 3+ weeks active | Sports Nutrition: Before-After |
| **STRONG** | 3+ competitors, 2+ weeks active | Cosmetics: Ingredient Focus |

**Traceable:** We can name exact brands using each hook (credibility).
