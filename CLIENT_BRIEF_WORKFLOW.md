# Client Brief Workflow — Step-by-Step Process

**Purpose:** Turn client brief → 5 hook variations → delivery  
**Timeline:** 48 hours  
**Output:** 5 videos + hypothesis doc + testing protocol

---

## 📋 PHASE 0: Client Brief Collection (BEFORE 48h Sprint)

### Step 0.1: Send Brief Template to Client

**Via Email/Calendar Link:**

```
Subject: Let's create your 5-video sprint test

Hi [Client Name],

Quick brief (2 minutes to fill):

1. What's your product? (1 line)
   Example: "Protein powder for fitness"

2. Who's your ideal customer?
   Example: "Gym-goers 25-40, looking for muscle"

3. What problem do they have (that your product solves)?
   Example: "Slow muscle recovery after workouts"

4. What ads are WINNING right now?
   (Share 1-2 screenshots of your best-performing ads on Meta)

5. Your Meta Budget? (Helps us scope testing)
   Example: "€500/week testing budget"

Reply to this email, and we'll kick off your sprint Monday morning.

[Your name]
```

**Expected Response Time:** Same day or next morning

### Step 1.2: Find Competitors on Meta Ad Library

**Process:**

```
1. Go to facebook.com/ads/library
2. Select: France, Active ads, Videos
3. Search for: [Client's 3 main competitors]

For each competitor:
- Note: # of active ads
- Filter: Ads running 3+ weeks (these are winners)
- Screenshot: Top 3 most-engaged ads
- Analyze: What hook is each using?
```

**Template — Competitor Analysis Sheet:**

```
CLIENT: Respire (Natural Deodorant)

COMPETITOR 1: Lamazuna
- Active ads: 12
- Oldest ad duration: 4 weeks
- Top ads hooks:
  1. "Before-after body transformation" (4 weeks) ← BEST
  2. "Health benefit claim" (3 weeks)
  3. "Customer testimonial" (2 weeks)
- Pattern: Before-after is WINNING

COMPETITOR 2: Schmidts
- Active ads: 8
- Oldest ad duration: 3 weeks
- Top ads hooks:
  1. "Scientific proof" (3 weeks)
  2. "Happy customer" (2 weeks)
- Pattern: Science + testimonial mix

COMPETITOR 3: Nudi
- Active ads: 7
- Oldest ad duration: 4 weeks
- Top ads hooks:
  1. "Problem-solution" (4 weeks)
  2. "Lifestyle" (2 weeks)
- Pattern: Problem-solution strong

WINNING HOOKS PATTERN:
✅ Before-After (4 weeks, most engagement)
✅ Problem-Solution (4 weeks, solid engagement)
⚠️ Science/Testimonial (2-3 weeks, growing)
```

**Time:** 20 min per client

### Step 1.3: Cross-Reference with Hook Sheets

**Action:**

```
From HOOK_SHEETS_BY_SECTOR.md:
- Get sector (e.g., Cosmétiques)
- Read top 5 hooks for that sector
- Compare: Do competitors validate these hooks?
- Confirm: Which hooks are actually winning for THIS client?
```

**Template — Hook Validation:**

```
CLIENT: Respire

HOOK_SHEETS suggests for Cosmétiques:
1. Before-After Skin (BEST)
2. Ingredient/Science Focus (STRONG)
3. Problem-Solution (STRONG)
4. Unboxing/Aesthetic (MODERATE)
5. Testimonial/Real User (MODERATE)

META AD LIBRARY validates:
✅ Before-After: YES (4 weeks, most engaged)
✅ Problem-Solution: YES (4 weeks, solid)
⚠️ Science: PARTIAL (2-3 weeks only)
❌ Unboxing: NO (not found)
⚠️ Testimonial: PARTIAL (2 weeks)

CONCLUSION: Focus on Before-After + Problem-Solution
Alternative: Add one Science angle for variety
```

**Time:** 10 min

---

### Step 1.4: Research TikTok Trends (Optional but Valuable)

**Action:**

```
Search TikTok for:
- #[product category] trending
- #[pain point] solutions
- Filter: 1M+ views

Look for: What hooks are VIRAL right now?
These often appear on Meta 4-6 weeks later.
```

**Example:**

```
TikTok search: #deodorant natural
Top videos (1M+ views):
- "I tried 5 natural deodorants" (comparison hook)
- "Before-after 48h test" (transformation hook)
- "Doctor reaction to natural deodorant" (expert hook)

Insight: Comparison angle is trending → Add as 5th variation
```

**Time:** 10 min (optional)

## 📝 PHASE 2: Strategic Planning (Day 1, 2pm-5pm)

### Step 2.1: Define 5 Hook Variations

**Process:**

Based on Meta Ad Library findings + Hook Sheets + TikTok trends:

**Template — 5 Hook Variations:**

```
CLIENT: Respire
SECTOR: Cosmétiques
MAIN PAIN POINT: Natural deodorant as alternative to chemical

HOOK 1: BEFORE-AFTER TRANSFORMATION (Competitor-validated, 4 weeks)
- Visual: Close-up armpit before → after 48h use
- Emotion: Relief, confidence
- Duration: 10-12 sec
- Best for: Problem-solution clarity
- Why: 4 weeks active in competitors, most engaged

HOOK 2: BEFORE-AFTER EMOTIONAL (Variation of #1)
- Visual: Person feeling embarrassed → relieved
- Emotion: Shame → pride
- Duration: 8-10 sec
- Best for: Emotional connection
- Why: Same hook, different angle

HOOK 3: PROBLEM-SOLUTION SPEED (Competitor-validated, 4 weeks)
- Visual: "48-hour test" format
- Emotion: Quick win, easy solution
- Duration: 10-12 sec
- Best for: Urgency, fast results
- Why: Complementary to transformation, tested in market

HOOK 4: SCIENCE/INGREDIENT AUTHORITY (Secondary hook)
- Visual: Expert/dermatologist explaining
- Emotion: Trust, credibility
- Duration: 12-15 sec
- Best for: Skeptics, premium positioning
- Why: Validates natural claims, builds trust

HOOK 5: COMPARISON (TikTok trend, emerging)
- Visual: "This vs That" (chemical vs natural deodorant)
- Emotion: Smart choice, informed
- Duration: 10-12 sec
- Best for: Conversion of skeptics
- Why: Trending on TikTok, differentiator vs competitors

RATIONALE:
- Hooks 1-3: Proven winners from competitors
- Hook 4: Trust builder, secondary hook from sheet
- Hook 5: Forward-thinking (emerging trend)
```

**Time:** 20 min

---

### Step 2.2: Write Hypothesis Document

**Template:**

```
# Sprint Test Hypothesis — [Client Name]

**Sprint Date:** [Date]
**Product:** [Product]
**Testing Budget:** [Budget] (e.g., €200-500)
**Duration:** 7 days

## The Five Variations

### Variation 1: Before-After Close-Up
**Hook:** Visual transformation (armpit before → after)
**Hypothesis:** "Direct visual proof of deodorant effectiveness 
  will convert skeptics fastest"
**Why This Works:** 
- Competitor [Lamazuna] ran this 4+ weeks (proven)
- Before-after is most-engaged format in category
- Clear pain point → solution
**Success Metric:** Highest CTR day 1-3
**Best If:** Audience values visual proof

### Variation 2: Before-After Emotional
**Hook:** Emotional journey (embarrassment → confidence)
**Hypothesis:** "Emotional before-after resonates better than 
  pure product transformation"
**Why This Works:**
- Same proven hook as #1, but emotional angle
- Cosmetics category thrives on emotional sells
- Personal story > product shot
**Success Metric:** Highest engagement rate (comments, shares)
**Best If:** Audience values relatability

### Variation 3: 48-Hour Speed Test
**Hook:** Problem-solution with urgency ("Test 48h")
**Hypothesis:** "Speed positioning differentiates us from 
  traditional deodorant testing cycles"
**Why This Works:**
- Competitor [Schmidts] validates speed hook
- "Quick test" creates urgency
- Performance/speed is unique angle for Respire
**Success Metric:** Highest conversion rate (link clicks)
**Best If:** Audience values innovation

### Variation 4: Science/Expert Authority
**Hook:** Dermatologist explaining natural benefits
**Hypothesis:** "Expert credibility builds premium positioning 
  and converts luxury-minded audiences"
**Why This Works:**
- Secondary hook from Hook Sheets (STRONG rating)
- Validates "natural" claims with authority
- Separates from competitors (most don't use this)
**Success Metric:** Highest conversion from cold traffic
**Best If:** Audience values health/safety

### Variation 5: Comparison (Natural vs Chemical)
**Hook:** Side-by-side comparison (natural deodorant vs chemical)
**Hypothesis:** "Direct comparison highlights unique value prop 
  and converts on differentiation"
**Why This Works:**
- Trending on TikTok (#deodorant videos)
- No competitors currently using this
- First-mover advantage on this angle
**Success Metric:** Emerging engagement indicator
**Best If:** Audience values informed choice

## Testing Protocol

**Budget Allocation:**
- Variation 1 (Before-After CU): €50 (control)
- Variation 2 (Emotional): €50
- Variation 3 (Speed): €50
- Variation 4 (Science): €40
- Variation 5 (Comparison): €30

**Duration:** 7 days (full learning cycle)

**Success Criteria:**
- At least 1 variation hits 2%+ CTR (benchmark)
- Top performer identified by day 3-4
- Clear winner = scale for next 2 weeks

**Next Step (Day 7 Debrief):**
- Review which variation won
- Scale top 2-3 performers
- Design next sprint around winner

---

**Created by:** Timeline-AI  
**Date:** [Today]
```

**Time:** 15 min

---

### Step 2.3: Write Testing Protocol Doc

**Template:**

```
# Testing Protocol — [Client Name]

## Setup (Do This First)

1. **Create test ad account** (if needed)
   - Budget: €200-500
   - Duration: 7 days
   - Audience: [Client's typical target]

2. **Set KPIs to track:**
   - CTR (click-through rate)
   - CPC (cost per click)
   - CPM (cost per thousand)
   - Conversion (if applicable)
   - CAC (cost per acquisition) if selling

3. **Create conversion tracking** (if selling)
   - Add pixel to landing page / cart
   - Track purchase if applicable

## Daily Tracking

### Day 1-2: Monitoring & Learning
- Check all 5 variations are live
- Screenshot CTR/CPM for each
- Note which one is leading
- NO PAUSES YET (need data)

### Day 3-4: Early Winner Detection
- Top 2 variations clear
- Bottom 2 falling behind
- Optional: Pause lowest performer
- Allocate budget to leaders

### Day 5-7: Scale & Confirm
- Top performer clear
- Consider scaling budget on winner
- Gather all data for debrief

## Debrief (Day 7)

**What to Analyze:**
1. Which variation had highest CTR?
2. Which had lowest CPC?
3. Which would scale best?
4. Learnings for next sprint?

**Next Iteration:**
- If top performer: Scale it
- Create 5 new variations around winner
- Test again (48h sprint next week)

---

**Contact:** [Your email]
**Support:** Available for questions anytime
```

**Time:** 10 min

## 🎥 PHASE 3: Video Creation (Day 2, 9am-6pm)

### Step 3.1: Prepare Video Briefs

**For each of 5 hooks, write a brief:**

```
VARIATION 1: Before-After Close-Up

Product: Respire deodorant
Duration: 10-12 seconds
Format: Vertical (9:16 for Meta/TikTok)

Hook (0-2s):
- Close-up of armpit "sweaty/smelly" (exaggerated/humorous)
- Text: "The problem with normal deodorant"

Story (2-6s):
- Quick shot of Respire product
- Text: "Respire: Natural alternative"
- Show application (quick)

Result (6-10s):
- Same armpit close-up "fresh/dry"
- Text: "48 hours later... still fresh!"

CTA (10-12s):
- Product shot
- Text: "Natural deodorant that works"
- Button: "Try risk-free for 30 days"

Tone: Humorous, relatable, quick
Music: Upbeat, modern
```

**Time:** 5 min per hook (25 min total)

---

### Step 3.2: Create Videos in HeyGen

**Process for each video:**

```
1. Open HeyGen.com
2. Create new video
3. Paste script/brief
4. Select avatar + voice
5. Add visuals/text overlays
6. Generate video
7. Download MP4
```

**HeyGen Settings (Template):**
```
Avatar: Professional, friendly
Voice: Native language (French for FR market)
Background: Clean/relevant to product
Text overlays: Bold, readable
Music: Trending (upbeat for deodorant, calming for sleep, etc.)
Duration: Exactly 10-12s per spec
Format: 9:16 (vertical)
```

**Output:** 5 MP4 files (1-2 MB each)

**Time:** 2-3 hours (HeyGen processing time included)

---

### Step 3.3: Edit & Polish (Optional)

**If needed (e.g., add titles, music, transitions):**

```

Process:
1. Import HeyGen MP4
2. Add title/branding at start
3. Add music (royalty-free)
4. Add text overlays
5. Export as final MP4
```

### Step 5.2: Plan Next Sprint

**At Debrief, Agree On:**

```
1. Scale winner to full budget
2. Create next 5 variations (based on learnings)
3. Schedule next sprint test (next week)
4. Define success metrics for next sprint
```

**Example:**

```
This Week: Emotional Before-After winning at 2.95% CTR

Next Sprint Hypothesis:
"Can we beat 2.95% by testing variations of emotional hook?"

New 5 variations:
1. Emotional + faster pacing (2x speed)
2. Emotional + different age group (teen vs adult)
3. Emotional + humor (comedic relief angle)
4. Emotional + aspirational (future self)
5. Emotional + community (group testimonial)

Test budget: €500
Test duration: 7 days
Goal: Find 1-2 variations that beat 2.95%

---
```
